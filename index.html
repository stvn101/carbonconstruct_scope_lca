About CarbonConstruct

Built by Construction Professionals, for Construction Professionals

CarbonConstruct was born from frustration with complex, expensive carbon calculators that weren't designed for Australian construction realities. The purpose of CarbonConstruct is to make embodied carbon measurement accessible and practical for SME's and construction professionalsin the construction industry.

Founded in 2025 by Steven Jenkins, with 17 years of tier 1 experience working on the biggest commercial jobs in Brisbane, I have a unique understanding of what needs to be done and I know how to fix it. With EC3 integration SME's now have available to them a powerful tool to measure and reduce embodied carbon in their projects.

I understand the pressure you're under: tight deadlines, budget constraints, and increasing regulatory requirements. That's why we built a tool that's fast, accurate, and actually useful for real-world construction projects. CarbonConstruct isnt just another piece of software-it's a game changer for how we approach sustainability in construction.
